Hey, how does this competition work?
Basically, you form a team of 3 to 5, then register on this website. You can browse through the event list to find out events or tasks that you can participate in to gain points for your team. Participate in the event, enter the password and recieve the points against your teams name. After 24 hours, the competition is up, and the team with the most points is the winner.
Have fun!
